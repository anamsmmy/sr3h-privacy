🔒 Privacy Policy

SR3H Converter

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

Introduction

We value your privacy. This policy explains how the "SR3H Converter" app handles your data.

1. Data Collection

Our app is 100% local and collects no personal data

  • ✅ We never send videos to external servers
  • ✅ We don't collect personally identifiable information
  • ✅ We don't send usage analytics
  • ✅ We don't track your location
  • ✅ All processing happens locally on your device

2. Required Permissions

The app requests the following permissions only for functionality:

  • Photo Library Access: To select videos to convert
  • Camera Access: To record new videos (optional)
  • Microphone Access: To record audio (optional)
  • File Access: To save converted videos

Important Note: These permissions are only used on your device and are not shared with any third parties.

3. Security

  • ✅ The app does not connect to the internet (except for optional subscriptions)
  • ✅ All processing happens locally on your device
  • ✅ Videos are not stored in the cloud
  • ✅ Your data is 100% secure

4. Subscription (RevenueCat)

If you choose to subscribe to the premium plan, payment data is processed through:

  • RevenueCat - for subscription management
  • Apple / Google - for payment processing (we never receive your card data)

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. You will be notified of any significant changes through the app or email.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • 📧 Email: 7jgamer.ads@gmail.com

7. Your Rights

  • ✅ You have the right to delete your subscription data
  • ✅ You have the right to cancel your subscription at any time
  • ✅ You have the right to control your device permissions
Thank you for using SR3H Converter ❤️
